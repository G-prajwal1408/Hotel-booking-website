About Lovely Hotels

Lovely Hotels are the group of hotels formed in 2010, Known for it best serviece. The main branch of Lovely Hotels are located in Jalandhar, Punjab, which is the shortest and equal distance from both amritsar and chandigarh airport. Jalandhar is an ancient city in the north Indian state of Punjab. Its home to Devi Talab Mandir, a centuries-old Hindu temple dedicated to the goddess Durga, with a tank considered sacred by devotees. The whitewashed Gurdwara Chhevin Patshahi is a place of worship for Sikhs. To the southeast lies St. Marys Cathedral, a modern building with Punjabi architectural elements, 2 domed towers and a pond. Probabily it is the paradice for the touristers.
